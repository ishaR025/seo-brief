## 5. Competitor Insights
- **Typical Coverage:** Top-ranking articles usually cover an overview of Beautiful.ai, list various alternatives with their features and pricing, and provide a comparison or guide on how to choose the best alternative.
- **Content Gaps:** Many articles lack in-depth analysis of the user experience, customer support, and the learning curve of these alternatives.
- **Unique Value Propositions:** Emphasize the importance of customization, collaboration features, and the ease of use for non-designers. Highlight any unique features or innovations that set certain alternatives apart.

## 6. On-Page SEO Checklist
- **Internal Linking:** Link to relevant articles on presentation design, software reviews, or productivity tools using generic anchors like "learn more" or "discover".
- **Image Alt Text:** Use descriptive alt text for images, including target keywords where relevant, e.g., "Beautiful.ai alternative presentation software screenshot".
- **Schema Markup:** Apply schema markup for software applications, including name, description, operatingSystem, and review ratings to enhance search result snippets.

## 7. Writing Guidelines
- **Tone and Voice:** Informative, neutral, with a hint of friendliness to engage readers.
- **Reading Level Target:** 8th grade to ensure accessibility for a broad audience.
- **Key Points to Emphasize:** The importance of evaluating features, pricing, and user experience when choosing an alternative to Beautiful.ai.
- **What to Avoid:** Directly comparing or demeaning Beautiful.ai, focusing too much on technical jargon, and overlooking the needs of non-designer users.
