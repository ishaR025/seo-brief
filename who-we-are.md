# Cuebo — Who We Are

## The Origin

We built the training every customer-facing team actually needs — but rarely gets.

Training hasn't changed much. Reps go through onboarding, shadow a few interactions, and then get pushed into real customer conversations. Feedback comes late. Coaching is inconsistent. The people who need the most help often get the least.

We've seen this across industries — deals lost, customers dropped, not because the product wasn't good, but because the rep wasn't ready.

So we built Cuebo.

A place where teams practice real customer conversations, get instant feedback, and improve every day — on their own time. Cuebo acts like a sparring partner that's always available. It tells reps exactly what went wrong and what to say differently, right after each attempt.

The result: faster ramp, better conversions, and more confident customer-facing teams.

If your team is still learning on live interactions and waiting days for feedback — it's time for something better. That's Cuebo.

## What We Are

**Cuebo is AI Roleplay with real customer personas, instant feedback and on-demand coaching — built for sales teams that want to close more.**

Not a training tool. Not an LMS. A revenue activation system.

The difference matters. Training software measures completion. Cuebo measures readiness — and readiness is the leading indicator for conversion that most revenue leaders don't have.

We give reps a place to practice real conversations under real pressure before they go live. And we give revenue leaders the visibility to know exactly who is ready to close and who isn't — before it shows up in the pipeline.

---

## Our Thesis

The traditional model of sales enablement treats training as an event.

One onboarding. One product launch. One annual refresher. Then reps are on their own, and managers are left coaching by gut feel from call recordings they don't have time to review.

The companies winning in sales right now treat readiness as a continuous process — not a one-time event. They build reps who practice the way athletes practice: repeatedly, against realistic resistance, with immediate feedback on what went wrong and what to do differently.

Cuebo is the infrastructure for that model.

Every simulation a rep runs feeds into how they perform on the next real call. Every real call surfaces new gaps to practice. The loop never stops — and the system gets better the more you use it.

## Core Beliefs

- **Readiness predicts revenue.** The quality of a rep's conversation is the most controllable variable in your pipeline.
- **Practice requires repetition under pressure.** Watching a recording is not practice. Having a hard conversation 20 times before it counts — that's practice .
- **The loop must close.** Training that doesn't connect back to real call performance is noise. The only training that matters is the kind that shows up in your numbers.
- **Managers shouldn't be the bottleneck.** When every rep needs a manager's time to improve, you've built a system that can't scale. Cuebo scales coaching without scaling headcount.
